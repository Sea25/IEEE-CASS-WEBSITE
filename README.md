A modern, responsive website for the **IEEE Circuits and Systems Society (CASS) Kerala Chapter**. This site serves as the digital presence for one of Kerala's leading technical societies, connecting professionals, researchers, and students in the circuits and systems domain.

## ✨ **Features**

### **Implemented**
- ✅ **Fully Responsive Design** - Seamless experience across all devices
- ✅ **Hero Section** - Engaging landing area with chapter stats (500+ members, 50+ events)
- ✅ **About Section** - Vision, mission, and technical focus areas
- ✅ **Events Showcase** - Upcoming and past events with registration CTAs
- ✅ **Team Grid** - Executive committee profiles with hover effects
- ✅ **Contact Form** - Interactive form with success feedback
- ✅ **Social Media Integration** - Links to all chapter social platforms
- ✅ **IEEE Brand Compliant** - Strictly follows CASS branding guidelines

### **Coming Soon** 🚧
- 🔄 Member registration portal
- 🔄 Research publication repository
- 🔄 Photo gallery from past events
- 🔄 Technical blog section
